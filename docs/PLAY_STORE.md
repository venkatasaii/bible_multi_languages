## Full description (4000 char limit) — paste verbatim

```
Read the Holy Bible (King James Version) anywhere — completely offline — and translate any verse into 14+ languages with one tap. Holy Bible by Kuchive Apps is a clean, distraction-light KJV Bible reader built for people who want scripture on their phone without needing an internet connection.

★ FULLY OFFLINE — The complete KJV Bible (all 66 books, Old and New Testament) is bundled in the app. Read on a plane, in the subway, in a tent, or anywhere without WiFi or mobile data.

★ TRANSLATE EVERY VERSE — Tap the language pill and pick from Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, Telugu, Tamil, and more. The translation runs on your device using Google ML Kit — your verses are never sent to a remote server.

★ BEAUTIFUL TYPOGRAPHY — Hand-tuned reading layout with classic Lora and Cinzel serif fonts, generous line spacing, drop-cap chapter openings, and a warm paper-and-ink palette designed for long reading sessions. Light and dark themes included.

★ SIMPLE NAVIGATION — Tap a book, pick a chapter, swipe forward or back. No social feed, no notifications, no devotional gimmicks — just the text.

WHAT'S INCLUDED
• All 66 books of the King James Bible (Old Testament + New Testament)
• Genesis to Revelation, every chapter, every verse
• Books grouped by Testament with short three-letter abbreviations
• Beautiful chapter headers with traditional ornament dividers
• Drop-cap first letter and superscript verse numbers in a flowing paragraph layout
• Translate every verse on demand — Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, Telugu, Tamil (more coming)
• On-device translation via Google ML Kit — no verse text ever leaves your phone
• Works fully offline once the app and any chosen language pack are downloaded
• Compact 35 MB install
• No account, no signup, no login

WHY HOLY BIBLE BY KUCHIVE APPS

There are many Bible apps. Most are bloated with social features, daily plans, devotionals, and feeds you didn't ask for. Holy Bible is for readers who just want the King James Bible text — quickly, cleanly, in good typography, and in the language they understand best. The on-device translation is the headline feature: read Genesis 1 in English, then translate the same chapter into Spanish or Hindi in a single tap, without ever sending your reading to the cloud.

PERFECT FOR
• Daily personal Bible reading
• Bible study and verse-by-verse meditation
• ESL learners reading scripture alongside their native language
• Missionaries and travelers without reliable internet
• Multilingual families who want to share verses across languages
• Anyone who wants a calm, ad-light KJV reader

THE KING JAMES VERSION (KJV)

The 1611 King James Version is the most widely read English translation of the Bible in history. It is in the public domain worldwide, which is why we ship the full text inside the app at no charge. Every word in Holy Bible is the unaltered KJV text.

PRIVACY

We do not collect your name, email, reading history, bookmarks, or any personal data. Translation happens on your device. Ads are served by Google AdMob using your device's advertising ID, which you control in Android Settings. Full policy: https://venkatasaii.github.io/bible_multi_languages/privacy.html

CONTACT

Suggestions, bugs, requests for new languages or Bible versions:
kuchiveapps@gmail.com
```
