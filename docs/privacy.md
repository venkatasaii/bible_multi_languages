# Privacy Policy — Holy Bible by Kuchive Apps

**Effective date:** 28 May 2026
**Publisher:** Kuchive Apps
**Contact:** kuchiveapps@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how the Holy Bible app ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use the application available on Google Play.

## Plain-English summary

- We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or account.
- We do not collect or transmit your reading history, bookmarks, or notes.
- The Bible text is bundled inside the app and read directly from your device — no internet required.
- When you tap **Translate**, your device contacts Google to download a small language model the first time, then runs translation locally on the device. The verse text is sent to Google's on-device library, not to a remote server.
- The app shows advertisements served by Google AdMob, which uses your device's advertising ID to serve ads. You can reset or limit this ID in your Android settings.
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## What we collect directly

**Nothing.** The app does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry SDKs that we control. We do not maintain user accounts.

## What third-party services receive

### 1. Google ML Kit (on-device translation)

When you select a non-English language in the Reader screen:

- **What is transferred:** The first time you pick a language, the Google ML Kit library on your device downloads a translation model (~30 MB) from Google's servers. Subsequent translations use the cached model and do not transmit verse text.
- **Where translation happens:** Locally on your device, using the downloaded model. Verse text is not sent over the network during translation.
- **Provider's policy:** [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) applies to the model download request.

### 2. Google AdMob (advertising)

The app displays banner and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob to support free distribution.

- **What is collected:** Your device's advertising ID, approximate (city-level) location derived from IP, device model, OS version, language, and ad interaction events. This is collected by Google, not by us.
- **Why:** To serve relevant ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.
- **How to control it:**
  - Reset or delete your advertising ID in **Settings → Privacy → Ads** on Android.
  - Opt out of personalized ads in the same screen.
  - In the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland, you will be shown a consent prompt the first time you launch the app and can choose what data Google AdMob may use.
  - California residents may exercise their CCPA rights via the in-app consent screen or by emailing kuchiveapps@gmail.com.
- **Provider's policy:** [Google's advertising privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## Permissions the app requests

- **Internet access (`INTERNET`, `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`)** — required to download translation models on first use and to serve ads. The app functions fully offline for reading once installed.

The app does not request access to your contacts, photos, microphone, location, storage, or any other sensitive permission.

## Data retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Anything held by Google AdMob or Google ML Kit is subject to their respective retention policies, linked above.

## Children

The app is rated for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app and we should be aware of something, contact us.

## International users

By using the app you understand that any data Google AdMob or Google ML Kit collects may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States. We do not control these transfers.
